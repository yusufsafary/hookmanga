01 — Executive Summary
What is HookManga?

HookManga is an AI-powered tool launched in 2026 dedicated to helping anime content creators generate viral short-form video hooks. Built for TikTok creators, manga storytellers, and anime fans with growing audiences, it combines AI-generated story hooks, character concepts, and video scene ideas into a single fast workflow.

This whitepaper outlines our vision, the problem we solve, our AI approach, tokenomics, and roadmap for the future.

02 — The Problem
Why Creators Struggle

Anime content creators face persistent challenges when trying to grow on short-form platforms:

  • Hook fatigue: Overused tropes and predictable openings result in low watch time and poor algorithm performance.
  • Time cost: Writing a compelling story hook from scratch takes 30–60 minutes per video — unsustainable for daily creators.
  • No structure: Most creators intuitively understand what makes a hook good, but lack a repeatable system.
  • Generic AI: Existing AI tools generate bland, culturally uninformed content that lacks the emotional language of manga storytelling.
03 — Our Solution
AI-Powered Manga Storytelling

HookManga addresses these gaps through a purpose-built AI system trained on the emotional architecture of manga storytelling:

  • Hook Engine: Generates emotionally charged story hooks in the exact format proven to drive curiosity on short-form video.
  • Viral Mode: Amplifies curiosity gap and emotional intensity using advanced prompt engineering.
  • Character Builder: Full character profiles with backstory, powers, and cinematic key scenes for series content.
  • Genre Intelligence: Separate AI prompts optimized for Dark, Romance, Shonen, and Isekai genres — each with distinct tone and tropes.
04 — Tokenomics
$HOOK Token on Solana

HookManga is powered by the $HOOK token on the Solana blockchain, listed on Orynth. The token creates a sustainable utility loop connecting creators and the platform:

  • Burn-to-generate: Each AI generation burns 1,000 $HOOK tokens from the user's wallet — creating constant deflationary pressure.
  • Token gating: Access to premium features (Viral Mode, Character Builder) requires holding $HOOK.
  • Creator rewards: Future feature to reward top creators with $HOOK airdrops based on community engagement.
  • Governance: Token holders vote on new genre presets, feature prioritization, and platform direction.

About

Why We Built This

HookManga was born from a simple frustration: the best anime content creators have incredible ideas, but the tools to express them virally didn't exist.

We are a team of manga fans, developers, and Web3 builders who believe that great storytelling should be accessible to every creator — not just those with screenwriting training or large production budgets.

Our mission is to become the essential tool in every anime creator's workflow — from the first spark of an idea to a polished viral hook, in seconds.

  • Creator-first: Every feature is designed around what actually performs on short-form video.

  • Web3 native: $HOOK token creates a real utility loop between creators and the platform.
